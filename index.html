How Prediction Markets Work.

1

Event Definition

Events with uncertain outcomes are defined, typically as binary (Yes/No) questions. e.g., "Will India's GDP growth exceed 7% next quarter?"

2

Trading Contracts

Participants buy or sell "Yes" or "No" shares. A "Yes" share pays $1 if the event happens, $0 if not. Prices range from $0.01 to $0.99.

3

Price = Probability

The share price directly reflects the market's collective belief in the probability. A $0.85 price means an 85% perceived chance.

4

Settlement

Once the event occurs, contracts pay out. Correct shares are worth $1; incorrect shares expire worthless. Profits are calculated accordingly.
